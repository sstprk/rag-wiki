Target Programs & Scholarships (2026/27 cycle):
1. Lithuania – KTU Data Science and Artificial Intelligence M.Sc.
   - Erasmus bonus (+0.5 pts), IEEE publication (+2 pts), GPA advantage.
   - Vise approval ~98.7%, no weekly work-hour limit.
   - State scholarships deadline: 20 Apr 2026.

2. Italy – Politecnico di Milano (PoliMi) Computer Science & Engineering / Informatics Engineering.

3. Hungary – ELTE Eötvös Loránd University Computer Science M.Sc. via Stipendium Hungaricum (full scholarship: tuition + stipend + housing + flight).
   - Deadline: 15 Jan 2026 (applications already open).
   - University entrance exam (algorithms/Python/math) instead of IELTS.

4. Portugal – Técnico Lisboa (IST) / FEUP Porto (Data Science & Engineering / Software Engineering).
   - Leverage current Lisbon internship for internal references.
   - Tuition 3–7k€/year; possible company sponsorship post-internship.

General advantages:
- MOI certificate from MCBÜ (English prep + curriculum) bypasses IELTS/TOEFL in all four countries.
- High acceptance probability (3.45 GPA + IEEE pub + KTU history).
- Post-grad work rights: 12 months (LT/IT/PT), 18 months (DE). Blue Card pathway strong for AI/ML/DS roles.
